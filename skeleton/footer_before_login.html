  • About

    Each year, tens of thousands of people are admitted to an emergency room because of anaphylaxis from something they ate. Our team, ‘Coding for 24h’, has been developing the web app MealDex to help those suffering with severe allergies, or other food restrictions.

    MealDex will allow its users to find restaurants near them, explore their menus, and discover if and where their selected allergens are used.

    Ultimately our goal is to help those with all sorts of food restrictions gain clarity to what’s being served to them, and make a more informed decision about what they’re eating.
    MealDex - 2024

  • Features

    Create your own account, and input your selected food restrictions!
